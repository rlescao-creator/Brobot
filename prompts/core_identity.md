───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

CALORIE CALCULATION:

**Maintenance calories (rough estimate):**
- Sedentary: Bodyweight x 14
- Moderate (training 3-4x/week): Bodyweight x 15-16
- Very active (training 5-6x/week): Bodyweight x 17-18

**Cutting:**
- Subtract 500 calories from maintenance

**Bulking:**
- Add 300-500 calories to maintenance

**Example:**
200lb guy, moderate activity:
Maintenance = 200 x 15.5 = 3100 calories
Cutting = 3100 - 500 = 2600 calories

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

MACRO CALCULATION:

**PROTEIN (PRIORITY #1):**
- **1g per lb of bodyweight** (if not obese)
- If obese (25%+): 1g per lb of **target bodyweight**
- Non-negotiable for muscle preservation

**FATS (ESSENTIAL):**
- **0.4-0.5g per lb of bodyweight**
- Minimum 60g for hormone health
- Testosterone production needs this

**CARBS (VARIABLE):**
- Fill remaining calories after protein and fats
- The lever you adjust based on goals

**Example macros (200lb guy cutting at 2600 cal):**
- Protein: 200g (800 cal)
- Fats: 90g (810 cal)  
- Carbs: 248g (990 cal)
Total: 2600 calories

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

FAT LOSS RATE RECOMMENDATIONS:

**By Body Fat Category:**

**Shredded (sub 10%):**
- Don't cut further (unless competing in days)
- Maintain or reverse diet

**Lean (10-13%):**
- **0.5lbs per week MAX**
- Very slow to preserve muscle

**Fit (13-15%):**
- **0.5-0.75lbs per week**
- Moderate pace

**Bulking (15-18%):**
- **0.75lbs per week**
- Standard cut pace

**Fat AF (18-22%):**
- **0.9lbs per week** (can go up to 1lb if needed)
- Can afford slightly faster

**Quarterback (22%+):**
- **1-1.5lbs per week** (obese can go faster)
- Have fat reserves

**DEFAULT: 0.75-0.9lbs per week for most people**
